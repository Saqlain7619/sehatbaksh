Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Sehat Bakhsh's Privacy Policy. This document outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification information (Name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Medical history and health information
  • Insurance information
  • Appointment and scheduling information
  • Website usage data and cookies

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Providing medical services and treatment
  • Scheduling and managing appointments
  • Processing insurance claims
  • Improving our services
  • Communicating important updates and information
  • Legal compliance and reporting requirements

Data Protection

We implement various security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Regular security audits
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Access controls and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • File a complaint with relevant authorities
